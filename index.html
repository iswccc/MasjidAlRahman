Family Night

The first Saturday of every month, join us for family night. Bring your family and friends for a night filled with spiritual nourishment followed by dinner.

Program begins after Asr
After School Program

Our program runs from Mon-Thur 4:30PM - 6:30PM for boys and girls ages 6 - 15, covering the following Islamic subjects:

  • Qur'an Recitation
  • Hifz (Qur'an Memorization)
  • Tajweed (Qur'an Prononciation)
  • Fiqh
  • Seerah
  • Adhaab and Aqhlaq (Behavior and Manners)
